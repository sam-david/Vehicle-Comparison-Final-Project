About Us

This site was created as a tool for Tesla owners, potential owners, and fans to see how the Tesla models stack up against other vehicles on the market across a wide array of statistics. Thanks to Edmunds.com's amazing public API, we are able to do this. We compare fuel, performance, and overall annual costs for Tesla vs any model on the market that Edmunds has data for. Try it out and see for yourself!
